## **Quiz**

- What is the difference between ***var*** and ***let***?
Let and var have different scopes. You can also redeclare with var and you can not with let.

- What is the difference between **var** and ***const***?
You can not redeclare or reassign with const.

- What is the difference between **let** and **const**?
You can reassign with let but not with const.

- What is hoisting?
It is the order of how the compiler works.
